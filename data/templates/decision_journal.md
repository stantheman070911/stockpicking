# Decision Journal Template

## Purpose
Create a one-page record of why the position was entered so later review can separate process quality from outcome noise.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Entry date:
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Position mode:
- <<REQUIRED>> Why now:
- <<REQUIRED>> Variant perception link:
- <<REQUIRED>> Sizing decision:
- <<REQUIRED>> Primary risk:
- <<REQUIRED>> Next review date:

## Guidance
- Keep this tied to the actual decision made at entry, not the full research archive.
- Link to supporting memos or templates when relevant.
- The next review date should match the expected cadence of the setup.

## Example
```md
Entry date: 2026-04-18
Stock / setup: 2330 after another strong monthly-revenue print.
Position mode: Tactical with compounder override.
Why now: Revenue inflection and packaging bottleneck relief align inside one review window.
Variant perception link: See variant_perception.md for the counter-consensus setup.
Sizing decision: Enter at half of the mechanical cap pending the next earnings call.
Primary risk: Consensus is right that AI upside is already fully reflected in mix assumptions.
Next review date: 2026-05-10 after monthly revenue.
```

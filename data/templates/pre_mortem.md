# Pre-Mortem Template

## Purpose
Assume the position failed and work backward before entry so latent risks are surfaced early.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Decision under review:
- <<REQUIRED>> Assume it is 12 months later and the position failed:
- <<REQUIRED>> Top 3 failure causes:
- <<REQUIRED>> What signal we likely missed:
- <<REQUIRED>> Preventive action now:

## Guidance
- Write in complete sentences, not fragments.
- Focus on plausible failure paths rather than tail-risk fiction.
- Preventive actions should be things we can actually do before or during the position.

## Example
```md
Stock / setup: 3443 on handset-cycle recovery.
Decision under review: Initiate a starter position ahead of the next product cycle.
Assume it is 12 months later and the position failed: Design-win optimism arrived a quarter early and margin leverage never showed up.
Top 3 failure causes: End-demand miss, customer concentration, and inventory build hidden by channel stuffing.
What signal we likely missed: Sell-through weakness at key OEM customers.
Preventive action now: Keep sizing small until two monthly-revenue prints confirm the recovery.
```

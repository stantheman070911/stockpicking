# Management Forensic Checklist Template

## Purpose
Guide the manual management-quality review that free-tier automation cannot yet complete for Taiwan issuers.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Capital allocation review:
- <<REQUIRED>> Incentive / proxy review:
- <<REQUIRED>> Candor test:
- <<REQUIRED>> Insider trading / share-pledge review:
- <<REQUIRED>> Open governance questions:

## Guidance
- Review multiple years of management communication where possible.
- Note what could not be verified from free-tier data.
- If share-pledging cannot be confirmed, mark it as manual follow-up rather than silently clearing it.

## Example
```md
Stock / setup: 2454 platform-cycle review.
Capital allocation review: Buybacks and capex have generally aligned with ROIC discipline.
Incentive / proxy review: Management pay appears tied to operating metrics, but proxy detail is incomplete.
Candor test: Commentary on inventory digestion has been consistent across earnings calls.
Insider trading / share-pledge review: No clean free-tier data source; manual TWSE / MOPS review required.
Open governance questions: Need confirmation on director pledging and related-party exposure.
```

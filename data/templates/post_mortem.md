# Post-Mortem Template

## Purpose
Review an exited position on process terms so the framework improves with repetition instead of relying on memory.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Exit date:
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Outcome versus thesis:
- <<REQUIRED>> Process grade:
- <<REQUIRED>> What was right:
- <<REQUIRED>> What was wrong:
- <<REQUIRED>> What rule changes:

## Guidance
- Grade the decision quality separately from P&L.
- Write at least one rule or checklist update if the process missed something.
- If no rule changes are needed, say why the existing process was sufficient.

## Example
```md
Exit date: 2026-09-01
Stock / setup: 2317 AI server basket.
Outcome versus thesis: Partial win; revenue came through but the multiple compressed sooner than expected.
Process grade: B
What was right: The supply-chain read and sizing discipline were sound.
What was wrong: We underweighted customer concentration and timing risk around the earnings call.
What rule changes: Require explicit customer concentration commentary in the position-sizing template for ODM names.
```

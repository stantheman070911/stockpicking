# Scuttlebutt Call Log Template

## Purpose
Provide a structured, compliance-aware record of channel checks, expert calls, or fieldwork linked to a thesis.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Contact type:
- <<REQUIRED>> Date:
- <<REQUIRED>> MNPI cleared:
- <<REQUIRED>> Key takeaways:
- <<REQUIRED>> What changed in thesis:

## Guidance
- If the source was not pre-cleared, stop and document that no call should proceed.
- Record only usable, non-MNPI observations.
- The last line should state whether the call changed the thesis, sizing, or nothing.

## Example
```md
Stock / setup: 2308 advanced-packaging demand.
Contact type: Former supplier sales manager.
Date: 2026-04-18
MNPI cleared: Yes; discussion stayed at industry-level observations.
Key takeaways: Packaging demand remains tight and customers are prioritizing high-end AI programs.
What changed in thesis: Increased confidence in near-term utilization but no change to governance view.
```

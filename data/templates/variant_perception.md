# Variant Perception Template

## Purpose
Capture the explicit market-implied view versus the analyst view so the memo states the edge, not just the research.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Market expectation:
- <<REQUIRED>> Analyst thesis:
- <<REQUIRED>> Error type {behavioral / analytical / informational / technical}:
- <<REQUIRED>> Evidence gap:
- <<REQUIRED>> What would prove the market right instead:

## Guidance
- Write the market expectation as the consensus or implied setup, not as a straw man.
- Name only one primary error type unless two are inseparable.
- The evidence gap should be the specific fact pattern that the market is underweighting or misreading.

## Example
```md
Stock / setup: 2330 after monthly-revenue inflection.
Market expectation: AI demand is already fully priced and gross margin has peaked.
Analyst thesis: Advanced packaging bottlenecks ease faster than expected, extending mix improvement into the next two quarters.
Error type {behavioral / analytical / informational / technical}: analytical
Evidence gap: Consensus models understate CoWoS capacity expansion and the operating leverage tied to that mix.
What would prove the market right instead: Capacity expansion slips and incremental AI demand is absorbed by pricing concessions.
```

# Invalidation Template

## Purpose
Pre-commit the non-negotiable conditions that would break the thesis so the exit decision is not improvised later.

## Required Fields
- <<REQUIRED>> Stock / setup:
- <<REQUIRED>> Core thesis:
- <<REQUIRED>> Non-negotiable invalidation trigger:
- <<REQUIRED>> Soft warning signs:
- <<REQUIRED>> Monitoring data sources:
- <<REQUIRED>> Required response if invalidated:

## Guidance
- One hard invalidation trigger is better than a page of soft caveats.
- Warning signs should prompt review, not necessarily exit.
- Make the response operational: review, reduce, or exit.

## Example
```md
Stock / setup: 2881 in a steepening domestic-rate backdrop.
Core thesis: Wealth-management fee recovery plus resilient capital ratios support rerating.
Non-negotiable invalidation trigger: Fee income and capital strength deteriorate together for two quarters.
Soft warning signs: Foreign selling, reserve pressure, or policy tightening hitting equity-linked product demand.
Monitoring data sources: Monthly revenue commentary, quarterly financial statements, institutional flow.
Required response if invalidated: Cut to tracking position and rewrite the thesis from scratch.
```
